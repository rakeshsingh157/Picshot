About Our Project

PicShot was born in a university classroom as a passion project by a group of computer science and photography students. We wanted to create a space that celebrates real photography in an age of AI-generated content.

Our platform is designed to be simple, authentic, and focused on the art of photography. We're committed to keeping PicShot ad-free and community-driven.
